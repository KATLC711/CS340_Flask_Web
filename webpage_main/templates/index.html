Welcome to Quality Auto Sales. We are the #1 dealership in Oregon. As an employee you have access to the following records:

  • Customer: Stores the customer's information

  • Sales Representatives: Stores the sale representative's information

  • Financial Arrangement: Stores the finacial agreement available to the customer, such as interest rates and number of payments

  • Vehicle Type: Stores the basic specifications of the vehicle available in the dealership

  • Monthly Payment: Stores the record of the monthly payment made by the customers

  • Vehicle Inventory: Stores the current inventory of the vehicles available in the dealership and where the vehicles are located in the dealership parking lot

  • Sales Records: Stores the sales record of the dealership, including the invoice number, date, vehicle information, price and finance option

  • Test Drive Record: Stores the record of vehicles being taken out for test drive

  • Cashflow Projection: Displays the cashflow for a date range
